◆ spi_dev_t

typedef enum spi_devices spi_dev_t

SPI driver for the ESP-EDU Board.

Nota
ESP-EDU only have 1 SPI port, than can be connected up to 3 diferent devices using chip select pins (CS1, CS2, CS3). The SPI_0, SPI_1 and SPI_2 defines refer to this.
MISO: GPIO_22, MOSI: GPIO_21, SCLK: GPIO_20, CS1: GPIO_19, CS2: GPIO_18, CS3: GPIO_9

Albano Peñalva
