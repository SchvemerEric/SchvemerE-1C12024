◆ BUILT_IN_RGB_LED_PIN

#define BUILT_IN_RGB_LED_PIN   GPIO_8 /*> ESP32-C6-DevKitC-1 NeoPixel it's connected at GPIO_8 */

NeoPixel driver for the ESP-EDU Board.

Nota
This driver can handle only one stripe of NeoPixel at a time (with no limits in the qty of leds in the array).
ESP-EDU have one individual NeoPixel connected to GPIO_8, that can be used with this driver.
