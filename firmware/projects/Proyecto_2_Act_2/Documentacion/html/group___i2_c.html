◆ I2C_MASTER_SCL_IO

#define I2C_MASTER_SCL_IO   GPIO_7

I2C driver for ESP-EDU board.

Nota
SDA: GPIO_6, SCL: GPIO_7.
ESP-EDU have 4 I2C connector in the board (J4, J5, J6 and J8), but all of them are routed to the same I2C port.
